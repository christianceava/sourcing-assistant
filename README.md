# Sourcing Assistant

An online sourcing tool for your VAs. They click **5 / 10 / 20 leads** → it finds fresh Amazon ASINs that match the signature of your proven winners (Black Rifle Coffee, Huel, Activatedyou, Powerstep, Black Girl Vitamins, etc.) and returns them ranked with reasoning.

## What it does (in 4 lines)

1. The assistant has internalized your 365-day sales history (380+ ASINs across 9,300+ purchase rows).
2. From that, it knows what BSR / price / ROI / category / vendor signature predicts a winner.
3. When a VA clicks **Source N leads**, it queries the **Keepa Product Finder API** with filters derived from that signature, pulls full data on each candidate, scores them 0-100, and returns the strongest ones — excluding ASINs you already sell.
4. Every lead links straight to Amazon, Keepa, and SellerAmp for the VA to verify and source.

## To get this online (5 minutes)

See **[DEPLOY.md](DEPLOY.md)** — push to GitHub → connect to Streamlit Cloud → paste the Keepa key as a secret → deploy. App is live at a `*.streamlit.app` URL the VAs can bookmark.

## Scoring criteria & weights

| Criterion | Weight | What it checks |
|-----------|-------:|----------------|
| Sales rank (90-day) | 25 | Within winner BSR band — proxy for sales velocity |
| Sell price | 12 | Inside winner price range |
| ROI estimate | 18 | (sell − cost) / cost vs. winner ROI band (when cost provided) |
| Category match | 12 | Root cat is one we've won in |
| Live FBA offers | 8 | 3-12 = sweet spot, >20 saturated |
| Price volatility | 7 | Stable buy-box (low CV%) |
| Reviews & rating | 6 | 200-1000+ reviews, 4.0+ stars |
| Risk flags | 12 | Hazmat / oversize / Amazon on listing / adult |

Final 0-100 = weighted average. **≥80 BUY · 60-79 MAYBE · <60 SKIP.**

## Lite profile vs. full profile

The app deploys instantly using a **lite profile** built from `joined.csv` alone (sales + buy data — no Keepa prep). This is enough to get the BSR/price/ROI/vendor bands and the known-ASIN exclusion list working.

To upgrade to the **full profile** (better category targeting, brand patterns, risk flags), run `SETUP.bat` on your PC once. It'll pull Keepa for all 383 ASINs (~5 hours, resumable) and build `profile/winner_profile.json`. Push that JSON to GitHub and Streamlit Cloud auto-redeploys with the upgraded profile.

## Costs

- **Streamlit Cloud:** Free tier is fine for this.
- **Keepa API:** Each lead-source click consumes ~5 tokens (Product Finder query) + ~3 tokens per candidate detail pull. A 20-lead click costs ~125 tokens. Your refill rate determines pacing — check it in the sidebar.

## Notes on privacy

- The assistant does NOT surface your historical winners back as recommendations. Your sales/buy data is used internally to derive the winner signature; the VA only sees newly discovered ASINs.
- The `data/joined.csv` file does ship with the deployed app (it's the trained data). Keep the GitHub repo **Private** so your sales numbers stay confidential.
